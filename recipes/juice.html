Mulled Cranberry Juice

Description

This beverage is a good treat to get your guests in the holiday spirit. This is the non-alcoholic variant, sugar can be substituted for orange liquuer.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups cranberry juice cocktail
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 3 whole cloves
  • 1 cinnamon stick, plus more for serving
  • 1 or 2 small navel oranges, thinly sliced, plus more for serving

Steps

  1. Combine cranberry juice cocktail, granulated sugar, whole cloves, cinnamon stick, and orange slices in a 4-quart saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Bring to a boil; stir until sugar dissolves. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 10 to 20 minutes.
  3. Allow to cool slightly, and ladle into punch cups. Float one orange slice in each cup and garnish with a cinnamon stick. Serve warm.
